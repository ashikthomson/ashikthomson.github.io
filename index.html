Vision

We wish to improve the way of employment,thus making the finest talents across the globe to be accesible to all.

Our vision is that by 2025 outsourcing non-technical work will be as easy as contacting only a highly skilled creative director to get the work done by top talents scattered around the world.

Over the last decade, we’ve seen an increase in the amount of work outsourced online and more people freelancing through portals relating to non-technical work. These trends are set to accelerate now that a curated platform for freelancers to work under a creative director to complete quality projects from around the globe is in need.

Imagine if you could get your non-technical work to get delivered as if they were done with a team of your organization, while it's actually done by the top talents scattered across the globe. We’ll actually be able to deliver this as soon as we develop a platform where the customer can elaborately communicate with a creative director and then he initiates and completes the project through selected talents working at their own place and time zone.

Once this is possible, our addressable market suddenly opens up from smaller startups who require design or branding services to larger companies where highly funded advertising campaigns are done. This opens up a full time earning environment for our freelancers. Mostly like a branding/marketing company rooted all over the world.

Eventually, we can eliminate the situation where skilled minds leave behind all their creative talents to pursue a professional or technical education and thus to work in a typical corporate environment, because even before they are required to make a choice they could be earning well through our platform by working for big as well as small companies. And that’s a vision we believe is worth fighting for.
